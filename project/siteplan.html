Site Plan: Mayon Volcano

Site Purpose

This website aims to provide accessible and reliable information about Mayon Volcano, including its natural features, cultural significance, tourism highlights, and safety guidelines.

This site serves as a helpful resource for students, travelers, and anyone interested in learning about one of the Philippines’ most iconic volcanic landmarks. Located in the Bicol Region, it is part of the Pacific Ring of Fire. It is one of the most active volcanoes in the country, with over 50 eruptions recorded since 1616.

Despite its destructive potential, Mayon is also a popular tourist destination and a cultural symbol of the region. It attracts visitors for its scenic beauty and activities like ATV tours.
