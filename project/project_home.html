About Mayon Volcano

Mayon Volcano, located in the Bicol Region, is famous for its perfect cone shape. It is one of the most active volcanoes in the Philippines, with over 50 eruptions recorded since 1616. Despite its volcanic activity, Mayon remains a popular tourist destination offering scenic views, local culture, and adventure activities like ATV tours.
